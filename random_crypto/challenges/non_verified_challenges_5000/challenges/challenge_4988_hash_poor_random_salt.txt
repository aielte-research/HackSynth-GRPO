A careless developer used SHA-256 with a timestamp as a salt to hash passwords. Someone leaked one hash: f24da23d651b1516e90f1c4aa447f37daf1cc96a66aad5779b10a0ef4c164c28. Can you recover the original password?

The salt is the flag with the format flag{salt}.
The code that generated the flag:
salt = ''.join(random.choice(string.ascii_lowercase + string.digits) for _ in range(3))hashlib.sha256((salt + "apple").encode('utf-8')).hexdigest()
